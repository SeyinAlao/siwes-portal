What is SIWES?

Creation

How was SIWES created?

The Students Industrial Work Experience Scheme (SIWES) was established in 1973 by the Industrial Training Fund (ITF) to address the lack of practical skills among graduates of tertiary institutions for employment in Nigerian industries.

Founders

Who founded SIWES?

It was officially approved and presented by the Federal Government in 1974, with ITF as the founding organization responsible for its creation and initial funding.

The Aims & Objectives of the SIWES Progam

Our goal is to develop a centralized SIWES portal that enables students to seamlessly register, document, and manage their SIWES experiences in real time. This platform will empower students to stay organized, track their progress, and ensure their learning journey is both efficient and accessible on the go."

Cementing Your First Real Work Legacy The portal allows students to document their SIWES experiences in a structured way, creating a lasting record of their first professional engagements. This helps them reflect on their growth, skills acquired, and achievementsâ€”building a solid foundation for their career.

Easy Documentation With a centralized platform, students can effortlessly log their daily activities and experiences. It eliminates the hassle of maintaining physical records, making it simpler to stay organized and consistent throughout the program.

For Record Purposes The portal serves as a reliable archive for students, institutions, and employers to access SIWES records when needed. This ensures accurate tracking of participation, activities, and progress, which can be used for academic assessments or reference in the future.

For Research and Innovative Purposes The portal collects and organizes data from various industries and fields, creating a valuable resource for research and analysis. It promotes innovation by allowing students, educators, and policymakers to derive insights and develop strategies for improving industrial training programs.
